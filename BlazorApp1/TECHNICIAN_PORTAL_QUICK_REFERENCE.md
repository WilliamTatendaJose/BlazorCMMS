## ? Common Questions

**Q: How do I see work orders for other technicians?**
A: You can't. You only see your assigned orders.

**Q: Can I edit an order?**
A: No, only supervisors/admins can edit. You can only mark complete.

**Q: What if I make a mistake when completing?**
A: Contact your supervisor to revise the record.

**Q: How are performance stats calculated?**
A: Based on your completed work orders this week and on-time %, average labor hours.

**Q: Can I access this offline?**
A: No, internet connection required.

**Q: What should I fill in the completion form?**
A: Describe the work done, actual downtime, hours spent, and any parts used.
